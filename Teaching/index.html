COURSES

PHZ3150 - Introduction to numerical computing for STEM students

Learn how to:

  • program and do numerics and plotting in Python, NumPy and Matplotlib
  • visualize your data in 2D and 3D
  • plan and do your work and homework in your own computer
  • learn how to identify bugs in your code and fix them
  • learn how to make a good code that can be used by others

AST2002 - Astronomy

Understand the whys and the hows of:

  • day and night and seasonal cycles
  • our Solar system and other planetary systems
  • our Galaxy and other galaxies in the Universe
  • are we alone?

AST5334 - Exoplanets and Brown Dwarfs

This class aims to give the most up to date understanding of:

  • exoplanet and brown dwarf formation; similarities and differences
  • exoplanet migration
  • exoplanet population properties
  • exoplanet and brown dwarf atmospheres (radiative transfer, thermal structures, opacities, clouds and more)
  • dynamic evolution of exoplanets and evolution of exoplanet atmospheres
  • habitability
